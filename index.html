Introducing The Lads

We are Seb Jensen and Damian Rusecki from St Helena School, Colchester - Team 8346A, The Lads. We need your help to represent Essex and the UK at the world's most prestigious robotics event - the 2022 VEX Robotics World Championship presented by Northrop Grumman Foundation in Dallas, Texas, from 5-7 May. Recognised as one of the UK's top VEX Robotics Competition teams for exemplary engineering, we're determined to take the trip to the USA to show the world what we're all about.

    VEX Robotics

    The VEX Robotics Competition is a global challenge designed to engage students with the STEM fields (science, technology, engineering, and maths) across the world. Every season a new game is announced and teams are tasked with designing and documenting a competitive robot.
